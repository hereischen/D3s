The Scatter Chart

The scatter graph shows the whole data of chosen departments against the projectsâ€™ start date (X-axis in the format of YYYY-MM-DD) and completion date (Y-axis, in the format of YYYY-MM-DD), where the radius of the spots means the number of the life cycle cost of the each project.

A list of different circles represents different department, which can be clicked to filter the departments. The aim of this visualization is for the users to get the distribution graph based on start and completion date of various projects.

Single click a certain circle in the list to check or uncheck a department, the scatter chart will hide or display the corresponding circles of the data.

Double click the circle to select one department; the data graph about selected department will be displayed.

Hover over the different circles of the scatter chart, information of the start and completion date, agency name and the cost will be highlighted accordingly.


This graph is applied Nv3d APIs (http://nvd3.org/) and some functions are modified from the examples.
